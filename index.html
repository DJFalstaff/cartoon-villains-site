Modern Magick RPG

Modern Magick is our default world setting for our games. This dark fantasy horror module was crafted specifically for use on Foundry VTT.

Set in the fictional town of Bedlam MA, it seamlessly weaves together historical landmarks, eerie New England folklore, and tributes to the macabre works of Poe, Lovecraft, and King. All of it presented with a touch of Whedonesque wit and charm.

It explores themes of morality and mystery, challenging players to navigate the blurred lines between Good and Evil while uncovering the greater secrets of mages living within the realms of the Modern Magick universe.

Learn Foundry VTT

Stepping into a digital game for the first time can feel a bit like learning a new language, especially with a platform as powerful as Foundry VTT. While Foundry offers incredible features, it can be intimidating to look at a screen full of buttons and menus when you just want to play your character.

The Cartoon Villains are here to help! We break down the interface into simple, easy-to-follow steps designed specifically for players. Whether you are trying to figure out how to move your token, manage your inventory, or just roll a critical hit, we will walk you through the basics so you can focus less on the software and more on the story.
